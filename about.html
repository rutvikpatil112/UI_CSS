About Sahyadri Farms

Sahyadri Farms is India’s largest integrated platform for fruits and vegetables. We empower small-landholding farmers by providing access to markets, technology, and world-class infrastructure.

Founded with the mission of ensuring fair returns for farmers and safe food for consumers, Sahyadri operates with a deep commitment to sustainability, transparency, and innovation.

Our Vision

To build a transparent, sustainable and inclusive agri-value chain led by farmers and driven by technology, ensuring that everyone from the farm to the fork benefits.
